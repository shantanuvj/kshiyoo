An Investment Proposal & Financial Overview

Prepared for 5 Partners

The Opportunity

This proposal outlines the joint acquisition of the Kshiyoo Bungalow, a premium property located in Malavali, Lonavala.

The investment is structured as a partnership among 5 individuals, sharing costs and potential returns.

Lonavala is a high-demand location for vacation rentals, offering a strong potential for generating annual income.

Discussion & Next Steps

Key Takeaways:

  • Each partner has a one-time initial investment of ₹4 Lakhs.
  • The estimated net monthly cashflow per person ranges from a cost of ~₹3,500 to a profit of ~₹1,500 in the first year.
  • The project is expected to become profitable for all partners by Year 5, assuming modest annual income growth.
  • Achieving profitability depends on maintaining a strong annual rental income (above ~₹14.1 Lakhs).

Points for Discussion:

  • Strategy for managing the annual cash shortfall.
  • Approach to marketing and managing the property to maximize rental income.
  • Policy for personal use of the bungalow by partners.
  • Formation of a formal partnership agreement.
