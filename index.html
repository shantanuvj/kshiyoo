The Opportunity

This proposal outlines the joint acquisition of the Kshiyoo Bungalow, a premium property located in Malavali, Lonavala.

The investment is structured as a partnership among 5 individuals, sharing costs and potential returns.

Lonavala is a high-demand location for vacation rentals, offering a strong potential for generating annual income.
